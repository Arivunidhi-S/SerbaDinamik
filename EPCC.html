EPCC

You are here: Services / EPPC

Serba's business unit offers the following services:

1. Engineering, Procurement, Construction and Commissioning (EPCC)

SERBA have the capability to undertake medium size projects such as pipelines, compressor stations, pump facilities, on shore production facilities, on shore marine terminal. However, we are able totake on bigger value projects in collaboration with our alliances. Our EPCC capabilities include civil works, planning, design, engineering, equipment supply, procurement, delivery, installation, testing, commissioning and the after sales service and maintenance of the complete project.

Our efforts in providing our expertise are backed by qualified and experienced professional Civil, Electrical & Mechanical Engineers specializing in their respective fields of Project Management Production, Erection & Commissioning. In association with our partners SERBA has established a regional network of customers for our EPCC services. While we’re expanding our range of services to take advantage of the growing energy market within the local and international market, we will ensure that our services will continue to better serve our clients with the highest quality in international standards.

2. Mechanical and Piping

Serba has a vibrant team of mechanical engineers and craftsmen who specialize in executing mechanical and piping projects. Such jobs consist of the installation of equipment (like towers, pumps and prefabricated units for instance), the installation and repair of storage tanks, the installation of structural steelwork such as pipe racks and platforms for example, and the fabrication and installation of industrial piping work.

We ensure that the necessary equipment such as cranes, welding machines, compressors and generators for example, are always available. Aside from that, Serba also employs specialized personnel to supervise heavy lifts and hire heavy-duty cranes able to lift loads of up to 1,200 tonnes from specialized companies for such purposes.

Serba also has a number of certified gridblasters and painters onboard our team to provide all sorts of coating work. That said, we always ensure that insulation, fireproofing and scaffolding jobs are always subcontracted to specialized companies.

Fabricated products are manufactured in accordance with the internationally accepted American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME), American National Standard Institute (ANSI) and American Petroleum Institute (API) standards. This quality of this service is further ensured by a series of inspection work and in-house testing before they are delivered or installed.
