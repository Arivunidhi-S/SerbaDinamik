Education and Training

You are here: Services / Training / Education and Training




SERBA is dedicated to enabling and assisting both individuals as well as organizations in enhancing their levels of productivity and quality.

We believe that ongoing learning - acquiring new knowledge, skills, attitudes and paradigms ‚Äì is essential for the survival and further development of an organization.

We recognize that organizations benefit from the integration of various levels, modes and types of learning, which provides focus and direction for productivity and quality enhancement activities.

Our training services are offered primarily through in-house workshops, which are customized to meet our clients' requirements. The training sessions may be conducted at the clients' premises or at an external venue selected by the client.

In addition, we offer public training programs on selected topics, which are publicized periodically.

We work closely with our clients to identify and analyze what their needs are, and then to design and develop appropriate training programs or management systems as required.

Our carefully selected panel of associates with specialized skills and experience enable us to ensure that our clients receive the most appropriate level of support.
