Global Trading Business Unit

You are here: Services / System Integrator / Global Trading Business Unit

Serba's business unit plays an integral role in our Integrated Engineering, Contracting and Maintenance team, offering potential clients with the provision of equipment along with the other necessary parts required that are included in the contract. This division is responsible for coordinating all the trading services within the group.

It provides procurement and supplementary services of a wide range of products, from simple replacement orders of maintenance materials to a comprehensive package of:

  1. Mechanical,
  2. Electrical,
  3. Instrumentation,
  4. Rotating equipment,
  5. Insulation,
  6. Line pipes,
  7. Structural,
  8. Valves,
  9. Telecommunication Equipment or systems,
  10. Safety and protection systems,
  11. Chemicals, and
  12. Office equipment supplies





    The range of products and services offered by Serba's business unit include:

  1. Instrumentation and Control Equipment
    2. Actuators, operators, analyzers, controllers, indicators, recorders, data transmission equipment, data presentation devices, testing equipment, and process control computers.

  2. Electrical Power Generation and Transmission
    3. Motors, motor controls, boilers, drivers, cables, conduit, lighting fixtures, rectifiers, bearings and brushings, turbines, switches, and electrical heating equipment.

  3. Liquid, Gas Air Handling Equipment
    4. Pipes, fittings, pumps, valves, tanks, steam traps, hoses, compressors, blowers, ducts, ventilation systems, seals, gaskets, and tubings.

  4. Solid Handling Materials
    5. Bins, packaging equipment, conveyors and elevators for bulk materials, coatings and forming equipment, weighing, and feeding equipment.

  5. Engineering Materials
    6. Aluminium, copper, nickel and its alloys, iron and its alloys, ceramics, composites, plastics, rubber, linings, refractory materials, steels, insulation, and lubricant.

  6. Process Chemicals
    7. Catalysts, biocides, antioxidants, water treatment chemicals, dispersing agents, desiccants, solvents, surface active agents, absorbents, and gases.

  7. Plant Maintenance and Personal Safety
    8. Cleaning equipment and services, safety equipment and services, fire protection, maintenance tools and equipment, and noise and vibration suppression equipment.
