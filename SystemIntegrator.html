Serba Dinamik Group with more than 21 years of seasoned experience with large pool of technical expertises and technology partners worldwide will be able to extend its services from single specific discipline into an integrated system solution provider / packager which combine various disciplines and application.

MOBILE POWER SYSTEM
Power wherever and whenever you need.

We represent Capstone Microturbine in the region to supply various sizes of power: 30kW, 65kW, 200kW up to 1MW configurations. In any area where energy is not readily available, and, reliable clean-and-green energy is a necessity, a variety of Capstone Power Packages is quickly ready for business whenever and wherever needed. All Capstone MicroTurbines operate (a) Continuously or On-Demand, (b) Stand alone or Grid Connect, (c) Individually or Multi-pack, (d) Near zero emission profile, (e) Run on a variety of fuels: Low or High Pressure Natural Gas, Biogas (landfill, wastewater treatment centers, anaerobic), Flare gas, Diesel, Propane, Kerosene.



Some installations reference:


Units 65 KW for metering system (Onshore)

1 Unit C65 C1D2 for Offshore application

C65 CHP Unit to produce electricity and heat to produce hot water



POWER BOOSTER
The technology that will dramatically increase gas turbine power output.

We represent a MeeFog™ inlet-air fogging system. Since 1990, over 850 gas turbine inlet cooling systems have been installed in power plants, processing plants and gas pumping stations worldwide. Each has realized significant gains in power output and cost savings.
