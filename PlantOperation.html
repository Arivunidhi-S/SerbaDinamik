Operation and Maintenance

The Best Solutions of Operation & Maintenance:


Serba offers the best solutions in the operation and maintenance of all types of installations. Examples of which include those in thermal power plants, cogeneration plants, water treatment facilities, LNG plants (both regasification and liquefaction), waste treatment facilities, hydroelectric plants, combined cycles, diesel plants as well as industrial installations.

SERBA can improve process efficiency, reduce maintenance costs and energy consumption, as well as dramatically extend equipment life.

Through our services, we can provide process efficiency, reduce maintenance costs and energy consumption, as well as dramatically extend equipment life for your plant.
Maintenance services may be either preventive or predictive, may be performed on routine, scheduled or unscheduled, and are aimed at preventing equipment failure or decline. All of our maintenance services are focused on increasing safety as well as the efficiency and safety of your plants.

  • Our advanced management portfolio incorporates the following:
    • Comprehensive asset management
    • Effective management systems
    • Best-practice operational procedures
    • Balanced proactive / preventive maintenance planning
    • Detailed O&M report structures
    • Continuous improvement processes

Serba encourages a close working relationship with our clients and we actively emphasize the importance of sharing management systems, operational techniques,, resources and techniques.

Our areas of expertise include:
1. Total Engineering and Maintenance (TEAM) for Rotating Equipment

TEAM is a Serba trademark, which combines human resources, tools, proven techniques, products and software developed through years of in-house R&D and experience to provide improved reliability, efficiency and availability to our clients.

  • Specializing in rotating equipment, the following are provided under our TEAM package:
    • Turbomachinery installation and commissioning services
    • Field overhaul services
    • Workshop repairs
    • Multi-OEM services

  • SERBA also provides:
    • Quality auditing services during the commissioning of turbomachinery, and during the shutdowns and startups involved in turnarounds. These services include Dynamic Design Verification (DDV), and incorporate advanced engineering techniques, namely the operating deflection shape analysis, modal analysis, and finite element analysis.
    • In-situ field vibration analyses or the troubleshooting of machinery problems.
    • In-situ balancing services for all kinds of rotating equipment.
    • Oil analyses
    • Infrared thermography services.
    • The supply and application of high performance reinforced coatings and linings.
